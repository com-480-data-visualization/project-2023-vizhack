Bar Race Chart

This interactive visualization displays tobacco usage data over time, separated by country and year. To enhance comprehensibility and cater to your specific interests, we've added a region and sex selection feature at the bottom of the chart. This allows you to choose a specific region such as the "high-income" super-region and a specific sex (male, female, or both), narrowing the focus of the data displayed. Along with the scrubber, which lets you manually control the year being displayed, you can deeply explore the progression of tobacco usage at your own pace. Drag the scrubber back and forth to animate the bar chart, and observe the shifts in tobacco use among the selected region's countries and the selected sex over the years. This interactive tool will greatly enhance your exploration and understanding of the tobacco use trends within these specific regions and among different genders.
