Zoomable Sunburst

The Zoomable Sunburst diagram introduces a hierarchical perspective to our dataset, categorizing data into super-regions and sub-regional countries. The size of each segment represents the number of smokers. Click on a segment to zoom in and explore data for that specific super-region or country. Click on the center to zoom out. This interactive visualization provides an engaging and multi-dimensional view of our dataset, allowing for a deeper understanding of the prevalence of smoking across different geographical regions.
