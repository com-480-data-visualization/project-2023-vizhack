World map

This map visualization provides a geographical perspective of daily smoking prevalence. The map is color-coded to depict the prevalence of daily smoking in different countries and regions. You can hover over each country to view its specific data. This visualization offers an immediate visual understanding of the global disparity in smoking prevalence, contributing to a more complete picture of the worldwide tobacco use situation.
